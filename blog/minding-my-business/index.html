Minding My Business

2024-09-21

It's been a while since my last update. Please read my revised About page.

Activities

When you ask the question, "What's Mike doing?" Remember this photo of me at home alone, doing things I enjoy on my computer. This is what I'm doing 99.99% of the time.

Blog

This website (michaeldouglas.xyz) was originally set up as my UX/UI Designer Portfolio when I was looking for a job.

Because I'm focused on running my own business I don't need a portfolio. So I've converted this site to a personal blog.

Social Media

I do not trust social media companies. They make money selling my information to advertisers and training their AI on my posts. That's why my Facebook is a shell. I've deleted everything from my profile. I don't post anything or follow people.

Most people post on Facebook for attention and validation. No judgment against anyone who likes or uses Facebook, but it's not for me. I use Facebook for the Marketplace, not for socializing.

Rarely, I'll go to a specific person's profile and catch up on their posts. I'm happy they are doing things they enjoy. Usually, the things people post don't appeal to me.

I don't have the Facebook app installed on my phone. I check Facebook through my browser once a week on Friday afternoon. Otherwise, I stay away from it. If I didn't use Facebook Marketplace I would delete my account entirely. I prefer email for communication.

Business

My main focus is on my business StarcoreLabs.com.

I've had a few clients but I still have a lot of work ahead of me learning marketing to advertise to the right audience. If you know anyone who needs UX/UI or Graphic Design services please send them to my business website. Thank you!

Hobbies

I've been tinkering with my computers, watching YouTube, watching Movies, and playing games. Nothing exciting. I prefer to relax at home alone. That's what I've been up to.
