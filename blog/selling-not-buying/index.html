Selling, Not Buying

2026-01-09

Back to List

I have spent many years working in customer service and sales. I understand sales tactics very well. Because of that, sales tricks do not work on me. I am old and autistic. Which means I am not motivated by status, pressure, greed, or fear like many buyers are.

I am here to sell my products and services, not to shop. My prices and terms are clear. This is not a negotiation. You can take it or leave it. Please do not waste my time trying to sell me anything. If I want to buy something, I will look for it on my own.

Do not turn conversations into sales pitches. If you try to sell me something during a message, comment, or casual chat, it will only make me dislike you and your product. A comment on social media is not an invitation to pitch me.

Career coaches, this applies to you as well. Please stop trying to sell me paid services. You cannot promise a real, stable job. Selling resume rewrites and profile “optimizations” to people who are struggling comes across as taking advantage of desperation, not offering real help.

It is completely fine if you do not want to buy what I sell. That is your choice. But do not turn around and try to sell me something instead. The answer will always be no.
