Resource Consolidation

2026-02-18

Here’s a quick update for those of you who follow this blog.

On 3/15/2026, the domain michaeldouglas.xyz is up for renewal. After giving it a lot of thought, I’ve decided not to renew it.

I originally created this site as a professional UI design portfolio for corporate job applications. Over time, my direction has shifted. My focus now is fully on growing my own business and building Starcore Labs.

Because of that, it makes sense to bring everything together in one place.

Over the next week, I’ll be moving this blog and related content to starcorelabs.com. My goal is to consolidate my work, ideas, and resources into a single, focused platform that reflects where I’m headed.

The michaeldouglas.xyz site will be taken down the day before 3/15/2026.

If you’d like to keep up with updates, new posts, and future projects, please follow along at starcorelabs.com. I have several improvements planned and I’m excited about what’s next.

As always, thank you for reading and have a great day!
