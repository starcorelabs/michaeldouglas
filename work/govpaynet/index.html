GovPayNet

 

UX/UI Designer

I worked for Aventiv on the GovPayNet project.

GovPayNet is an internal tool used to assign submerchant hardware and permissions to process payments. Along with other administrative features.

 

Problem

The company wanted to add new pages to the web app to provide new functionality to its users. The tool was originally built by a separate external development team and hadn't been updated in years. No design files were made during it's construction.

They needed a designer to recreate the application in design software and then add in the new screens while maintaining the existing interface design.

Solution

To tackle this challenge I was granted read-only access to the web app. I used a browser plugin to take full page screenshots of every page.

I imported the screenshots into my software and meticulously recreated the design. Then I added in the new screens based on the requirements in the JIRA tickets.

Once the designs were completed the internal development team was able to update the web app.
