About

Hello, I'm Michael.

Welcome to my blog where I write about my experiences, interests, and thoughts on various topics. I’ve included old posts from previous blogs.

Here’s a few things to know about me:

  • I have type 2 diabetes which limits the kinds of food I can eat.
  • I have severe allergies to mold, pollen, and pet fur which limits my activities.
  • I'm mildly autistic which means I don't pick up on some unspoken social cues.
  • I don’t smoke, drink, use drugs, consume caffeine or sugar.
  • I have a positive growth mindset where I’m constantly learning new things and creating solutions.
  • I’m an Atheist, which means I don’t believe in god, the supernatural, or anything without physical evidence.
  • I'm anti-political because as long as lobbying is allowed, my vote doesn't matter.
  • I use Stoicism to deal with people, focus on what I can change, and let go of what is out of my control.
  • I’m interested in science, technology, design, video games, TV Shows, and Movies.

I was a Front-End Developer who switched careers to UX/UI Design. During my last corporate design job, I focused on designing graphical user interfaces for web and mobile applications. I worked on the following types of projects:

  • Corporate websites
  • E-commerce platforms
  • Web and mobile applications
  • Single landing pages
  • Static sites
  • Graphics for markting

Unfortunately, in April of 2023, I was part of a mass layoff that eliminated my whole division including my direct manager and his boss the department manager.

After struggling and failing to find another corporate job for 6 months, I started my own online business. Now, I run Starcore Labs where I offer design-related products and services.

I hope you find my posts entertaining. Thanks for stopping by and have a great day!
