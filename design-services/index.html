Client Process

  1. Video Meeting - I schedule a video meeting to discuss your project goals.
  2. Contract Review - We review my pricing options and contract. Payments are processed online through Stripe.
  3. Trello - I create seperate workspaces for client projects. Client's break down their project into individual tasksand add them as cards to Trello. The arrange them by priorty from top to bottom.
  4. Tasks and Revisions - I work on each task one at at time. Clients get unlimited revisions on each task. Trello sends and email when a tasks is marked complete.
  5. Deliverables - Completed tasks include downloadable deliverables.
  6. Time Frame - Tasks are completed in 1 - 5 business days, depanding on the complexity of the request.

Design Process

  1. Discover and Empathize - During the video meeting we learn about goal and pain points.
  2. Define - During our discussion we clearly define the problem and requested solution.
  3. Ideate - I map out the project as a flow chart or user flow. Then create a few versions of wireframe or mockups to provide various options for you to choose from.
  4. Prototype - Once we've agreeed on the layout and placement of components I create a full-color prototype.
  5. Review - I hold another video meeting and we review the prototype together. Giving you one last chance to make any changes to the design.
  6. Deliver - Once you're happy with the final design I combine all the project files into a zip and add them to Trello for downloading.

Pricing

I offer two flexible pricing options:

  1. Project Based - After discussing the scope of the project and the requested time frame to complete it. I calculate a flat fee for the entire project. This is a good option for people who are decisive and know upfront what they want. Usually the fastest choice.
  2. Value-based Pricing - Value-based pricing is based on a percentage of the value you will receive from the completed design. It's useful for large projects or teams that must to hold meetings to make decisions.
