How it Works

  1. Free Consultation - We schedule a 30 minute phone call or video meeting to discuss your project goals and fill out a design brief.
  2. Contract - After the meeting I'll email you a copy of my contract and the design brief with a price quote. Please review it, sign it, send me a copy, and pay the quote to hire me for your project.
  3. Trello - For large projects I'll create a workspace for you. I'll help you break down your project into single tasks and add them as cards in Trello. Then you can arrange your tasks by priority from top to bottom.
  4. Tasks and Revisions - I work on each task one at a time. Trello sends you an email when a task is marked for review or complete. You get unlimited revisions on each task.
  5. Deliverables - Completed tasks include downloadable deliverables.
  6. Time Frame - Depending on the complexity of the request, tasks are completed in 1 - 5 business days.

Design Process

  1. Discover and Empathize - During our meeting, I learn about your goals and pain points. These are written down in a design brief.
  2. Define - My goal during the meeting is to clearly define the problem and the scope of the design solution.
  3. Ideate - I map the project as a flow chart or user flow. Then I make a few versions of wireframes or mockups to provide various options for you to choose from.
  4. Prototype - Once we've agreed on the layout and placement of components, I create a full-color prototype.
  5. Review - We plan another meeting to review the prototype together. This gives you one last chance to make any changes to the design.
  6. Delivery - Once you're happy with the final design, I complete the work and combine all the project files into a zip file and add them to Trello or DropBox for downloading.

Unlimited Revisions

I offer unlimited revisions of tasks within the narrow scope of service and time frame defined in the design brief. Usually, no more than 30 days from the beginning of the project. This means while I’m working on your project, I’ll change the design however many times you want, until you’re happy, or we reach the project deadline. If you want to make changes after the project ends, I can create a new design brief with a new quote.

Privacy

By default I keep your company name and project details private. After the project is completed I may request to include your project in my portfolio with a brief description, link to your site, and screenshots of the design work I've completed. This helps me showcase my work and attract new clients. However, many of my clients have requested to keep their projects private, which is completely fine.

No AI

I do not use any artificial intelligence tools in my design process. Everything I do is hand-polished pixels to ensure human-made quality.

Flat Rate Pricing

I don’t offer an hourly rate. Instead, I provide flat rate pricing per project that’s paid upfront before I start working. I do this to avoid invoicing and chasing clients for payments.
