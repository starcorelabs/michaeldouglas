Dream Job

Recruiters send me job descriptions for roles on a regular basis. Some are not inline with my skill set or career goals. I added this page to clarify what my dream job looks like.

Title: UI Designer, Principle Designer, Visual Designer, or Lead Designer
Location: 100% Remote
Duration: Full-Time Salary Position
Compensation: $60 hourly or $125K annually with PTO, vacation, 401K, and insurance.
Interview: Phone Call and/or Video Call

Summary:

I'm a former front-end developer who switched careers to 100% remote UI Design.
I have over 10 years of experience working in corporate environments.

I've worked on many types of projects. From large-scale eCommerce and corporate web applications. To single landing pages and static sites.

My focus is on designing user interfaces for web and mobile applications.

Company Requirements:

  • Management is focused on company goals and prefers outcomes over methods.
  • No sprints or time-boxing because they know how to plan out projects over time.
  • Uses software tools like a Knowledge Base and Teams chat to avoid time-wasting meetings.
  • Avoids adding more than one task per JIRA ticket to speed up productivity.
  • Does not require the Designer to write any type of code.
  • Does not allow "Design by Committee". Because, it is the job of the Designer to define the colors, fonts, and the layout of projects based on Stakeholder Requirements, User Research, Design Thinking, and Universal Principles of Design.
